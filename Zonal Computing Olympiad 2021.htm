Zonal Informatics Olympiad, 2021 (ZIO-2021)

The Zonal Informatics Olympiad, 2021 (ZIO-2021) will be held on Saturday, 19 December, 2020 at centres across the country. ZIO will be conducted in cooperation with TCS iON, a division of Tata Consultancy Services. Online registration is open from 31 October, 2020 to 7 December, 2020.

  • Exam centres
  • Individual registration
  • Exam details
  • Bulk Registration

Exam centres

Centres for ZIO-2021 will be arranged in the following 36 cities.

Allahabad, Amritsar, Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Bokaro, Chandigarh, Chennai, Cochin, Coimbatore, Dehradun, Goa, Gwalior, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Indore, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Kanpur, Kolkata, Kota, Lucknow, Mumbai, Nagpur, New Delhi, Patna, Pune, Raipur, Ranchi, Shillong, Surat, Tiruchi, Trivandrum, Varanasi. Visakhapatnam.

The address of the exam centre in each city will be announced shortly.

Individual registration

  • Register online on the TCS iON Digital Hub for the Indian Computing Olympiad. You need to first register within the Digital Hub. Click on "Guidelines" for details.

  • The registration fee is Rs 472 (Rs 400 + GST), to be paid online (internet banking, debit card, credit card).

  • After paying the fee, you will receive an acknowledgment by email/SMS. An activation code for the Digital Hub will be sent to the registered email address within 24 hours. This will be needed to access the practice test (see below).

  • Registration is open till 7 December, 2020.

  • Admit cards will be available through the Digital Hub approximately 5 days before the exam.

Exam details

  • ZIO-2021 will be administered as a computerized test, on the TCS iON platform. The question paper pattern will be the same as in previous years. You will work out the solutions using pen and paper and only submit your final answers. Instead of a printed question paper and answer sheet, you will read the question paper on the screen and type in your answers on the computer.

  • The syllabus for ZIO is the list of Basic Topics in the ICO Online Study Material

  • Previous question papers and solutions are available in the ICO Archives: ZIO-2003, ZIO-2004, ZIO-2005, ZIO-2006, ZIO-2007, ZIO-2008, ZIO-2009, ZIO-2010, ZIO-2011, ZIO-2012. ZIO-2013. ZIO-2014, ZIO-2015, ZIO-2016, ZIO-2017, ZIO-2018, ZIO-2019, ZIO-2020.

  • You can also find past ZIO question papers with solutions and explanations at CodeChef's ZIO site.

  • The TCS iON Digital Hub for ICO has past ZIO papers available as Practice Tests. You can attempt these tests through the Digital Hub to familiarize yourself with the interface that you will be using during the actual exam.

Bulk registration

  • Bulk registration is only available for registering 10 or more participants at the same time. Details will be announced shortly.

International Olympiad in Informatics, 2020

IOI-2020 was organized online by Singapore during the period 13–23 September, 2020. The team that represented India at IOI-2020 was as follows.

  • Rajarshi Basu, Bodhicarya Senior Secondary School, Kolkata
  • Shashwat Chandra, Delhi Public School, RK Puram, New Delhi
  • Udit Sanghi, Bhavan Vidyalaya, Panchkula
  • Kshitij Sodani, Delhi Public School, Gurgaon

Rajarshi Basu and Udit Sanghi won silver medals, while Shashwat Chandra and Kshitij Sodani won bronze medals.


Rajarshi, Shaswat, Udit, Kshitij

The Leader and Deputy Leader of the Indian contingent were Madhavan Mukund and K Narayan Kumar, both from Chennai Mathematical Institute, Chennai.

Indian National Olympiad in Informatics, 2020 (INOI-2020)

The Indian National Olympiad in Informatics, 2020 (INOI-2020) took place on Sunday, 12 January, 2020. 32 students have qualified for the International Olympiad in Informatics Training Camp, 2020 (IOITC-2020) to select the Indian team for the International Olympiad in Informatics, 2020 (IOI-2020).

Zonal Computing Olympiad, 2020 (ZCO-2020)

The Zonal Computing Olympiad, 2020 (ZCO-2020) was held at 35 citues across India on Sunday, 1 December, 2019. 853 students registered for ZCO-2020, of whom 586 wrote the exam.

158 students have qualified through ZCO-2020 for INOI-2020..

ZCO-2020 was conducted in cooperation with Codechef.

Zonal Informatics Olympiad, 2020 (ZIO-2020)

The Zonal Informatics Olympiad, 2020 (ZIO-2020) was held at 35 citues across India on Sunday, 10 November, 2019. 594 students registered for ZIO-2020, of whom 463 wrote the exam.

152 students have qualified through ZIO-2020 for INOI-2020..

ZIO-2020 was conducted in cooperation with TCS iON, a division of Tata Consultancy Services.
